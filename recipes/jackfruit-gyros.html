Jackfruit Gyros

Description

This greek-inspired recipe uses jackfruit to mimic the meaty taste of this popular street food.

Enjoy this delicious gyro wrapped in pita with tomato, onion, and tzatziki, or on top of Greek lemon rice.

Recipe taken from Lazy Cat Kitchen (click here)

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion or substitute shallots (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (diced)
  • 1/8 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp dry oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder or more to taste
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tin jackfruit
  • 2 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrum
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper or sub black
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Drain jackfruit and squeeze out water with a cheesecloth.
  2. heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add diced onions and fry until lightly caramelised. Add chopped garlic and fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add dry spices and stir to combine.
  4. Add tomato paste.
  5. Add jackfruit along with soy sauce, maple syrup and apple cidar vinegar. Cut with scissors any pieces of jackfruit that are too large.
  6. Adjust seasoning and simmer for another 10-15 minutes.
  7. Set oven to 200C/380F. Spread jackfruit on baking tray and bake for about 20-25 minutes.
