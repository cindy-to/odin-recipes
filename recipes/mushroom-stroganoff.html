Mushroom Stroganoff

Description

This is the BEST vegan mushroom stroganoff you will ever try! It's packed with umami, is super flavorful, and is so creamy you won't believe it's dairy-free and vegan. It's the ultimate comfort food!

This recipe is loosely adapted from Rainbow Plant Life (original recipe here)

Ingredients

  • vegan margarine or butter
  • ~500 grams of mushrooms (I used cremini)
  • 6 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme, roughly chopped or 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 1/2 cups mushroom broth or vegan beef broth
  • 2 Tbsp tamari or light soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine, or sub apple cidar vinegar (just a dash)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 12 ounces pasta of choice
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the mushrooms and chop into even slices.
  2. heat vegan butter on a large, heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until lightly browned. Add salt to taste.
  4. Reduce heat. Add garlic and thyme and cook for 2-4 minutes.
  5. While mushroom is cooking, in a medium bowl, whisk together broth, tamari, Worcestershire sauce, and flour.
  6. Deglaze pan with white wine and reduce to simmer until alcohol has evaporated.
  7. Pour broth mixture into pan and whisk to combine. Simmer and then add coconut milk, nutritional yeast, and paprika. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook pasta in a separate pot of salted water until al dente.
  9. Add Dijon mustard to sauce and stir. Add pasta and toss to coat.
  10. Top the pasta with any herbs or spices. Enjoy!
